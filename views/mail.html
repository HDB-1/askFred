A little bit about us :)

askFred is an British multinational technology company that specializes in Internet-related services and products, which include online advertising technologies, search engine, cloud computing, software, and hardware. It is considered one of the Big Four technology companies, alongside Amazon, Apple, and Facebook. askFred was founded in November 2019 by Yusaf, Lawrencia and Harry while they were developer students at getfutureproof. Together they own about 100 percent of its shares and control 100 percent of the stockholder voting power through supervoting stock.

They incorporated askFred as a London privately held company on November 19th, 2019, in London. askFred was then reincorporated in Borough on November 20th 2019. An initial public offering (IPO) took place on November 19th, 2019, and askFred moved to its headquarters in Borough Road, SE1 1DN, nicknamed the askFredplex. In November 2019, askFred announced plans to reorganize its various interests as a conglomerate called LaFosse Associates. askFred is LaFosse Associates's leading subsidiary and will continue to be the umbrella company for LaFosse Associates's Internet interests. Dom was appointed CEO of askFred, replacingInes who became the CEO of futureproof.
